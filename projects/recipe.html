Cookies

Ingredients Quantity
Chocolate Chip 500
Butter 1 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Brown Sugar 1 Cup
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons
Baking Soda 1 Teaspoon
Flour 3 Cups
Hot Water 2 Teaspoons

Cookies are one of the most delicious foods that are cheap and easy to make. With the ingredients on the left, you can make delicious home-made cookies for you and your friends. You can find the steps to making them below.

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degress fahrenheit, or 175 degrees celsius. This is the temperature required to bake the cookies effectively.
  • After preheating the oven, mix the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl until they are smooth.
  • The third step is to add the two eggs, one at at a time, and then adding the vanilla extract.
  • The fourth step will be to dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Dip the batter in the baking soda and apply some salt as well.
  • After this, the fifth step is to add the flour and chocolate chips into the bowl. Make sure to give it a good stir before placing it onto the pan that it will be baked with.
  • Finally, bake the cookies for 10 minutes until the edges are brown.Enjoy your tasty cookies!

French Toast

Ingredients Quantity
Thick slices of bread 6
Eggs 2
Milk 2/3 of a Cup
Vanilla Extract 1 Teaspoon
Cinnamon 1/4 Teaspoon

French toast is one of the most popular breakfast foods around the world. It goes great with fruit and eggs. The recipe for french toast can be found on the left, while the steps to make the french toast can be found below. Enjoy!

  • The first step is to beat the eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon togetherin a bowl. Make sure to give these ingredients a good mix for the best results.
  • The second step is to get a skillet over medium heat. Wait a few minutes to make sure that the skillet gets warm.
  • Finally, dunk the bread rapidly onto the bowl and place it on the skillet. Leave it cooking and take it out when you see that its edges are golden. Repeat with all remaining slices. Enjoy!

Pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 and 1/2 Cups
Milk 1 and 1/4 Cups
Baking Powder 3 and 1/2 Teaspoons
Egg 1
White Sugar 1 Tablespoon
Melted Butter 3 Tablespoons

Pancakes are one of the most popular, if not the most popular, breakfast foods around the world. Although making them requires a certain degree of skill, the recipe that I have provided makes cooking pancakes easy. You can find the ingredients for it on the left and the steps to make pancakes on the bottom. Enjoy!

  • In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and sugar together. Pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until the consistency is smooth.
  • Now, heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium/high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Make sure that the pancakes are brown on both sides and serve. Enjoy!
