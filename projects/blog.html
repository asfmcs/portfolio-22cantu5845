    • What I have Enjoyed

    What I have enjoyed when it comes to learning HTML/CSS is that you can make very different styles of websites, and that you can make them very personal. Also, I like the feeling that I get when I complete a project or when my code is working, I feel very satisfied. I think that this is something that motivates me while coding, and to me this type of motivation makes coding very enjoyable.

    Furthermore, I also enjoyed learning HTML/CSS as it taught me skills on a topic that I might potentially study one day (coding). The projects that we have had have also made the learning purposeful, and I also like them because they are meant to simulate real projects that you might have if you decide to become a programmer. Overall, learning HTML/CSS has been a very enjoyable experience for me and I'm looking forward to learning more aspects related to it in the future.

    How has this Changed Me?

    When I started coding, I noticed that it was something that I enjoyed and that it could be a potential career for me. I had always thought that coding wasn't really a career option for me, but after learning HTML/CSS it has become one of my top options. Now, I also feel that learning the basics about different topics is very important, as they can help you discover your own interests and lead you to study a carrer that you love.

    HTML/CSS has also changed the way that I look at browsers, websites, and the internet. Whenever I look at a Wikipedia page now, I imagine the code that was written to create it. This has also made me appreciate software that helps us create websites and webpages without having to code everything. I feel that if I hadn't learned HTML/CSS, my level of appreciation for these helpful pieces of software wouldn't be the same.

    Things that I am Looking forward to Learning

    One thing that I am looking forward to when it comes to coding is learning how to do more advanced things that are found in websites, such as animations. Even if I don't learn this at school, I would still want to learn it during my free time because I find it very interesting.

    Something else that I am looking forward to learn is more ways in which I can make my websites look nice. Right now, I try my best to make my website look good, but due to the limited information that I have I am not able to program a website that looks professional. However, I feel that the more I learn the better my websites are looking and that eventually I will reach the point that I want to reach.
